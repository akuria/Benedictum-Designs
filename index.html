About Me

I am a NorCal native and moved to San Francisco in 2009. I am an outdoor enthusiast who loves to take advantage of all that CA has to offer. I am an avid mountain biker. Mountain biking down the BoyScout trail in Pacifica is among my favorite places to ride. Also topping my list are backpacking in Tahoe and Sequoia National forests, off-roading in Hollister, or anywhere for that matter, and white water rafting on the American and Kern rivers. I also enjoy sports and rooting for local teams such as the 49ers and Giants.

I also love DIY projects. I love working on my Xterra and modding it to be better off road. I also like woodworking, and just working on random projects in my garage. Gardening is also a passion of mine and I love spending time in my garden growing veggies. I even built a Greenhouse where I grew tomatoes which is really tough to do in the Sunset.


The photos of me 4wheeling were taken on a wheeling and camping trip at Hollister OHV. There they have miles and miles of trails and even a couple obstical courses where I can test my skills and rig as well as drive through some big mud puddles.
